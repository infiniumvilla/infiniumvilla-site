Best of both worlds

Villa Infinium is a four-bedroom villa with infinity pool set above the ancient town of Klis, a historic stronghold dating back to Roman and medieval times. It offers panoramic views of Split, the Adriatic islands, and surrounding mountains. Close to city attractions yet in a peaceful setting, it truly combines the best of both worlds.

All bedrooms are en-suite with king-size beds, plus gym, games room, two garages and private outside parking. The second-floor terrace provides a magnificent 360° view.
